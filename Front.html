Co-Impact

Co-Impact is a global philanthropic collaborative that sources and supports locally rooted coalitions in the Global South to transform underlying systems and achieve impact at scale, with a core focus on advancing gender equality and women’s leadership. The organization brings together institutional and individual donors to provide large, flexible grants and technical assistance in the areas of health, education, and economic opportunity.

Climate Justice Resilience Fund

The Climate Justice Resilience Fund works to empower women, youth, and indigenous peoples on the front lines of climate change to create and share their own solutions for resilience. With a focus on East Africa, the Bay of Bengal, and the Arctic, the organization funds advocacy, coalition building, and outreach initiatives to ensure that community voices shape policy solutions, particularly in the areas of water access, food security and sovereignty, sustainable livelihoods, and climate-related migration and relocation.

Youth First Initiative

The Youth First Initiative seeks to accelerate reforms to end the use of incarceration of youth in the justice system. Through advocacy efforts and partnerships with various state groups, Youth First seeks to close youth prisons and invest in community-based alternatives. Youth First is increasing awareness about the negative impacts of youth incarceration; creating a national dialogue on alternatives to incarceration; working directly with youth, families, and allies on the local and national level to gather support for change; and supporting state initiatives with technical assistance.

About GoodGrowth

GoodGrowth is a crowdfunding platform dedicated to helping individuals and organizations raise funds for personal causes, health expenses, education, and more. We empower you to create the change you want to see in the world.


Our Mission

At GoodGrowth, we believe that everyone has the power to bring about meaningful change. Whether you're an entrepreneur with a groundbreaking idea, an individual in need of urgent medical assistance, or a student striving to achieve academic dreams, our platform is here to help you raise the funds you need. We connect passionate creators with compassionate supporters, fostering a space where great ideas can flourish and lives can be transformed.


What We Offer

  • For Project Creators: We provide an intuitive platform to share your story, set your goals, and reach a global audience. Our tools make it easy to track progress, engage with backers, and turn your vision into reality.
  • For Investors: Explore a diverse range of projects and causes that resonate with your values. Whether you're looking to support innovative startups, community initiatives, or charitable causes, GoodGrowth gives you the opportunity to make a real difference.

Why GoodGrowth?

We stand out for our commitment to transparency, trust, and success. Our dedicated support team is here to guide you through every step of your crowdfunding journey. With a focus on integrity and a passion for change, we ensure that every project on our platform has the best chance to succeed.
